## Core Beliefs & Philosophy

### What is Shamanism?
> "Shamanism is a spiritual practice that is not religiously affiliated with any one belief system. It is the oldest known spiritual healing system on the planet dating back 40-100 thousand years, based on archaeological studies."

### Origins of Shamanism
- Siberia is the home of the oldest shamanic tribe
- The word "Shaman" comes from the Tungus tribe in Siberia
- **Meaning:** "One who sees in the dark"
- During Soviet Union era, shamans were murdered and persecuted
- Some escaped to US where Michael Harner (founder of Foundation for Shamanic Studies) learned from them
- Harner used his organization to help re-establish their homeland after fall of USSR

### Universal Heritage
> "If you go back far enough, every culture on the planet came from tribes or villages that practiced shamanism, though called different things."
