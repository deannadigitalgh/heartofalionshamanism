About the Teacher, Deanna Stennett
Deanna Stennett, L.Ac., MA, Nationally Certified, NCCAOM, is a Georgia licensed
Acupuncturist with offices in Ellijay and Blairsville. She graduated from The Worsley
Institute (aka The Academy for Five Element Acupuncture) in 1993, and was practicing
in Maryland until 2021 when she moved to GA. Deanna learned about Shamanism in
Acupuncture School from a shaman, herbalist and acupuncturist by the name of Eliot
Cowan, author of Plant Spirit Medicine. After acupuncture school she began
studying with the Foundation for Shamanic Studies and completed their advanced 3
year program. She has also studied with Sandra Ingerman (author of many books,
including Soul Retrieval, and Medicine for the Earth,) since 1995.
Deanna has been practicing and teaching Shamanism since 1995. She offers classes,
workshops and healing sessions. Deanna has used Shamanic Journeying to meet with
the spirits of the Acupuncture Points which her book Poetry of the Body: Stories
About Acupuncture Points, focuses on. It is available on Amazon and is written for the
lay person.
Deanna Stennett is a lover of animals, nature, natural healing, art, poetry, and being a
loving, compassionate healing presence on the Earth.
