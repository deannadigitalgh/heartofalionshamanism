### 2. Messages from Nature Workshop

**Description:**
Explore how the Creator speaks through Creation. Using intuition combined with shamanic journeying to receive love and wisdom from nature spirits. You'll never look at a tree, flower, or bird the same way again.

**Key Message:**

**Focus Areas:**
- How the Creator speaks through Creation
- Using intuition combined with shamanic journeying
- Receiving messages from nature spirits

- Connecting with trees, flowers, birds, and all of nature

**Taglines:**

- "Out of the Dark into Light - Explore the gifts of both."

### 3. Wild Women Series

**Description:**
A six-part series designed for women to awaken their wild, intuitive, creative self and discover the joy of being a woman. Based on Clarissa Pinkola Estes' work, particularly *Women Who Run With the Wolves* and *Dangerous Old Women*.

**Philosophy:**
> "Deep inside you is a woman who longs to be free, wild, creative, imaginative and appreciated for those qualities."

**What You'll Explore:**
- What it means to be wise as you are
- Old age as a privilege with much to offer younger generations
- The special power of women to form healing friendships
- Awakening wildness and allowing it to be free
- Using wisdom that only women have
- Connecting with intuition through shamanic journeys
- Creative expression (song, dance, artwork, ceremony)

**Target Audience:**
Women of all ages - old, young, or in-between. Bring friends, daughters, sisters, mothers, or grandmothers to explore inner worlds together!

## Workshop Philosophy & Approach

### Core Beliefs
- **Universal Heritage:** "If you go back far enough, every culture on the planet came from tribes or villages that practiced shamanism, though called different things."
- **Nature Connection:** Focus on messages from nature spirits and living in harmony with the world
- **Women's Wisdom:** Special emphasis on women's unique wisdom and power
- **Community Building:** Creating healing friendships among participants

### Workshop Experience
- Interactive learning through shamanic journeying
- Creative expression (song, dance, artwork, ceremony)
- Community building and friendship formation
- Outdoor ceremonies when possible (fires, nature settings)
- Food sharing and community meals
