# Books & Publications: Poetry of the Body

## Main Publication

### **Poetry of the Body: Stories About Acupuncture Points**

**Author:** Deanna Stennett, L.Ac., MA  
**Format Available:** Paperback and Kindle on Amazon  
**Target Audience:** Lay people (written for general public, not just practitioners)  

---

## Book Concept & Content

### Core Theme
The book contains shamanism and acupuncture intertwined through personal stories. It explores:
- Deanna's personal stories from her practice
- Stories from patients she has treated
- How these stories relate to specific acupuncture points
- Connections between acupuncture points, seasons, and nature
- Shamanic journeying experiences meeting with the spirits of acupuncture points

### Unique Approach
Deanna has used **Shamanic Journeying** to meet with the spirits of the Acupuncture Points, which forms the foundation of this book. This represents a unique integration of:
1. Traditional Five Element Acupuncture (Worsley Institute training)
2. Shamanic spiritual practice (30+ years experience)
3. Narrative medicine (patient stories and experiences)

---

## Book Significance

### For Clients & Patients
- Written in accessible language for lay people
- Helps patients understand the deeper meaning behind acupuncture treatments
- Connects physical healing with spiritual/emotional dimensions
- Shows how nature and seasons relate to body's energy systems

### For Practitioners
- Demonstrates integration of shamanic principles into acupuncture practice
- Provides case studies showing patient experiences
- Offers alternative perspective on traditional acupuncture points
