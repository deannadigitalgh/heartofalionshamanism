Dee’s Shamanic Training and Experience
Training

in Mexico with the Huichol Indigenous people. Eliot taught my whole acupuncture
class how to do a shamanic journey.
1995 went to Tuson, AZ to do a learn to journey class with Sandra Ingerman, author
of Soul Retrieval, Medicine for the Earth: How to Transform Personal and
Environmental Toxins, Shamanic Journeying, How to Heal Toxic Thoughts, How
to Thrive in Changing Times, Awakening to the Spirit World: The Shamanic Path,
The Book of Ceremony, Welcome Home.
